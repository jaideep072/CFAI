Algorithmic Pathfinding

Visualize classic search algorithms like A*, Breadth-First Search (BFS), and Uniform Cost Search (UCS) in real-time. Watch as they explore frontiers, navigate around custom obstacles, and compute optimal routes using heuristics.

Immersive 3D Topology

Break the boundaries of 2D grids. Wrap your custom mazes mathematically around 3D primitives like Spheres, Cylinders, and Cubes. Watch the Pac-Man agent travel across planetary surfaces to reach its goal.

Constraint Satisfaction

Once a path is found, apply complex CSP algorithms to the solution. Run 3-Coloring engines to ensure no adjacent nodes share the same state, or schedule waypoints chronologically without conflicts.

Adversarial AI & Tracking

Place an adversarial Ghost on the grid. Play Minimax games to outsmart your opponent, or restrict your vision and use Hidden Markov Models (HMM) to calculate probability distributions of the ghost's hidden location.
